The Journey

My path into software development began in an unconventional way. Despite studying mechanical engineering at K.L. University, my fascination with computers guided me toward a specialized course in Data Structures and Algorithms during my third year. This unexpected detour changed everything, igniting a passion that would define my professional journey.

Through dedicated self-study and countless late nights of practice, I became the only mechanical engineering student in my class to secure a software developer position after graduation. This transition shaped my unique approach to problem-solving—combining analytical engineering thinking with creative coding solutions.

Working at Beamx Techlabs as a Full Stack Developer has been a journey of continuous growth. From building RESTful APIs to designing distributed systems, each challenge has pushed me to expand my knowledge. The blend of practical implementation and theoretical exploration keeps me energized and constantly curious about the next frontier in technology.
